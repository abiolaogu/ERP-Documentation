## 1. Getting Started with AI in Your ERP

ERP-AI provides intelligent assistance across every ERP module. You interact with AI through three main interfaces:

### 1.1 Copilot (Inline AI)

The Copilot appears automatically as you work in any ERP module:

- **Autocomplete**: Start typing and see AI-powered suggestions
- **Smart Defaults**: Forms pre-populate with intelligent defaults based on your history
- **Next-Best-Action**: Recommendations appear based on current context
- **Anomaly Alerts**: AI flags unusual patterns in your data

### 1.2 Natural Language Interface

Type questions in plain English anywhere you see the AI assistant icon:

- "Show me overdue invoices over $10,000"
- "Summarize last month's sales performance"
- "What is the churn risk for Acme Corporation?"
- "Generate a follow-up email for lead John Smith"

### 1.3 AI Insights Panel

Every module includes an AI Insights panel showing:
- Predictions and forecasts
- Anomalies and risks detected
- Optimization recommendations
- Confidence scores for each insight

## 3. Understanding AI Confidence

Every AI suggestion includes a confidence score:

| Score | Meaning | Recommended Action |
|---|---|---|
| 90-100% | Very high confidence | Safe to accept |
| 75-89% | High confidence | Review briefly |
| 50-74% | Moderate confidence | Review carefully |
| Below 50% | Low confidence | Verify independently |

---

## 4. Human-in-the-Loop

Some AI actions require your approval:

1. You will receive a notification (in-app, email, or Slack)
2. Review the AI's proposed action and reasoning
3. Click **Approve** or **Reject**
4. If you reject, provide a reason (this improves future AI accuracy)

---

## 5. Privacy and Safety

- AI never accesses data outside your authorized scope
- Your interaction data is never shared with other tenants
- AI-generated content is always marked as AI-generated
- You can disable AI features in your user settings
- All AI actions are logged for compliance
