# Data Room Checklist (Institutional Standard)

## 1. Corporate and Legal

- Certificate of incorporation and amendments
- Cap table (fully diluted, options, warrants, convertibles)
- Board and shareholder consents
- Material contracts and customer agreements
- IP assignments, trademarks, and patent filings
- Litigation and dispute disclosures

## 2. Financial and Accounting

- Historical financial statements (monthly and annual)
- Latest management accounts and forecast vs actual
- 5-year integrated financial model (driver-based)
- Revenue recognition policy and deferred revenue schedule
- Cash flow statements and runway analysis
- Tax filings and nexus documentation

## 3. Commercial and GTM

- Pipeline by stage, segment, and owner
- Cohort retention and expansion analysis
- Sales productivity metrics (ramp, attainment, conversion)
- CAC, payback, and LTV methodology with data support
- Top customer concentration and contract terms
- Churn analysis with root causes and remediation plan

## 4. Product and Technology

- Product roadmap with release history
- Architecture diagrams and scalability plans
- API documentation and service dependencies
- Reliability metrics (uptime, latency, incident history)
- Technical debt register and remediation roadmap
- Security architecture and secrets management approach

## 5. Security and Compliance

- Security policies and incident response plans
- Pen-test reports and remediation evidence
- SOC2/HIPAA/GDPR control mapping and status
- Access control matrix and privileged access logs
- Data retention and deletion policies
- Business continuity and disaster recovery evidence

## 6. People and Organization

- Org chart and leadership bios
- Hiring plan and compensation bands
- Employee option plan and grants schedule
- Attrition metrics and retention strategy
- Key-person risk and succession plan

## 7. Governance

- Board decks (last 4-6 quarters)
- KPI dashboard and definitions
- Risk register and mitigation tracking
- Internal controls and approval matrix

## 8. Fundraising-Specific Artifacts

- Executive summary
- Pitch deck
- Use-of-funds milestone plan
- Exit scenario model and return analysis
- Investor FAQ responses

## 10. Document Hygiene Standards

- Every file dated and versioned.
- Single source of truth for each metric.
- No conflicting KPI definitions across documents.
- Restricted access by diligence stage and data sensitivity.
