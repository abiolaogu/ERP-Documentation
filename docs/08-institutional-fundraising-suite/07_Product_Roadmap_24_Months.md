# Product Roadmap (24 Months)

## Roadmap Principles

- Every roadmap item must tie to measurable revenue, retention, or margin impact.
- Reliability and security are release-gated requirements, not backlog items.
- Product bets are sequenced to support GTM narrative and enterprise procurement.

## Quarter-by-Quarter Plan

| Quarter | Major Releases | Commercial Impact | Technical Impact |
|---|---|---|---|
| Q1 | Workflow orchestration v2 | Stronger demo conversion, faster onboarding | Better state management and event consistency |
| Q2 | Role-policy simulation and admin controls | Accelerates enterprise trust and procurement | Policy engine hardening |
| Q3 | Analytics and KPI command center | Improves expansion and executive adoption | Query optimization and metrics governance |
| Q4 | Integration marketplace pack 1 | Reduces implementation friction | Connector framework stabilization |
| Q5 | Intelligent exception handling | Higher workflow completion rates | Model-inference orchestration |
| Q6 | Predictive workload optimization | Upsell into performance module | Forecasting and optimization services |
| Q7 | Multi-region governance and resilience | Enables geographic expansion | Regional failover and data boundary controls |
| Q8 | Autonomous operations assistant | Premium module monetization | Cross-service automation and audit traceability |

## Epics by Strategic Theme

### Theme A: Core execution reliability

- deterministic workflow state transitions
- retry and rollback-safe automation
- environment-aware release controls

### Theme B: Governance and trust

- tenant and org policy inheritance
- role editor with simulation mode
- evidence-grade audit exports

### Theme C: Intelligence and optimization

- anomaly detection for process bottlenecks
- recommendation layer for next-best action
- outcome benchmarking across workflows

## Delivery Cadence and Resourcing

| Team | Scope | Sprint Cadence | Success KPI |
|---|---|---|---|
| Platform | reliability, performance, infra | 2 weeks | p95 latency, error budget |
| Product apps | UI and workflow modules | 2 weeks | feature adoption, task completion |
| Data and AI | analytics and optimization | 2 weeks | recommendation precision |
| Security and compliance | controls and evidence | 2 weeks | control coverage and audit readiness |

## Dependency Map

| Dependency | Blocking Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| Core event model maturity | Feature delays across roadmap | Early schema freeze and versioning policy |
| Policy engine stability | Enterprise rollout risk | Progressive rollout with feature flags |
| Data pipeline quality | Analytics trust degradation | Data quality SLOs and monitoring |

## Roadmap-Linked Commercial Targets

| Milestone Window | Product KPI | Business KPI |
|---|---|---|
| Months 0-6 | 65% feature activation in new accounts | win rate +3 pts |
| Months 7-12 | admin policy usage in >=70% enterprise accounts | sales cycle -10% |
| Months 13-18 | analytics dashboard weekly active usage >=55% | expansion ARR +20% |
| Months 19-24 | optimization module attach rate >=25% | NRR >=120% |

## Governance

- Quarterly roadmap review with board observers.
- Monthly KPI review tied to release outcomes.
- Formal de-scope mechanism for low-impact items.
