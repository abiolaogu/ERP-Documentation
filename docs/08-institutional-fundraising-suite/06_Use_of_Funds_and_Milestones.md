## 5. Milestone-to-Valuation Bridge

| Milestone | Value Creation Mechanism |
|---|---|
| Enterprise logo acquisition velocity | Supports top-line credibility and forward revenue multiple confidence |
| NRR >120% trend | Proves expansion economics and reduces dependence on new logo spend |
| CAC payback compression | Demonstrates scalable GTM efficiency and cash discipline |
| Gross margin expansion | Increases operating leverage and strategic optionality |
| Compliance maturity | Expands procurement access and lowers enterprise risk discount |

## 6. Downside Protection Controls

- Spend release tied to milestone achievement, not calendar alone.
- Rolling 13-week cash forecast with executive variance ownership.
- Hiring gates tied to pipeline quality and forecast confidence.
- Capex and non-critical initiatives deferred under downside scenario.

## 7. Board Reporting Template

Monthly board packet includes:

- Use-of-funds actual vs plan by bucket
- Milestone status heatmap
- Runway estimate under base/downside
- Recommended allocation adjustments

This structure ensures capital is used as a strategic instrument with measurable return impact, not as a general operating subsidy.
