# Risk and Mitigation Framework

## 1. Risk Governance Method

- Probability and impact scored 1-5.
- Composite risk score = probability x impact.
- Owners assigned at executive level.
- Mitigations tracked with due dates and verification evidence.

## 3. Top 5 Priority Risks (Near-Term)

1. Sales cycle elongation
2. Burn-rate deviation from model
3. Platform reliability under accelerated onboarding
4. Competitive pricing and feature bundling pressure
5. Compliance readiness for enterprise procurement

## 4. Mitigation Playbooks

### Commercial slowdown playbook

- Rebalance spend toward highest-converting verticals
- Shift quota mix to expansion where payback is superior
- Increase partner-originated pipeline percentage

### Reliability stress playbook

- Freeze non-critical releases
- Prioritize SRE and platform stabilization sprint
- Enforce incident postmortem action closure within 14 days

### Burn control playbook

- Pause non-core hiring
- Phase discretionary GTM programs
- Recast runway under downside and base scenarios weekly

## 5. Board-Level Risk Reporting

Monthly board risk packet includes:

- Risk score trend by category
- Mitigation completion status
- Emerging risks and trigger indicators
- Financial impact estimate per high-risk item

## 6. Trigger-Based Contingency Framework

| Trigger | Threshold | Action |
|---|---|---|
| Pipeline coverage | <1.8x next-quarter target | activate demand and partner acceleration plan |
| CAC payback | >24 months for 2 consecutive quarters | reallocate channel spend and pricing intervention |
| Gross margin | <75% for 2 quarters | infra optimization program and support model redesign |
| Service availability | <99.9% monthly | release freeze and reliability remediation sprint |

This framework is designed to convert risk from a narrative concern into an operating control system.
